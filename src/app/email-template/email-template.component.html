Raise The Bar

“Raising The Bar” is Diageo’s global programme to support pubs and bars as you welcome customers back following the COVID-19 pandemic. We want to stand beside you every step of the way.

“Raising The Bar” in GB, will work in two ways. Any bar anywhere in the world will have free access to Diageo Bar Academy where you can access best practice training and how to optimise your recovery.

you need to re-open including:
